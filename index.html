Team

CHRISTINE SU | CEO & Co-Founder

Christine Su is CEO and co-founder of Summer Technologies. She is completing a joint-degree MS in Land Use and Agriculture and an MBA at Stanford. She has worked as a ranch hand on a family-owned dairy in Japan, a hazelnut nursery in Asia, and Earthbound Farm in California. Christine started her career and McKinsey and KKR, where she built production planning dashboards for an Apple supplier and sales management systems for a $2 billion car retail franchise. Last summer, Christine built operations dashboards for a 10 million tree hazelnut nursery and for a mobile field reporting app to capture data for 15,000 orchards across rural Bhutan.

JENNIFER TSAU | CTO & Co-Founder

Jennifer Tsau is CTO and co-founder of Summer Technologies. She is currently completing an MS in Civil Engineering in Atmosphere and Energy at Stanford University. Jennifer has won a Grand Student Prize award for a Department of Energy Apps for Energy competition to help users understand the vague energy concept more clearly through games, and interactive displays. Prior to Stanford, Jennifer worked at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, and Northrup Grumman. She has experience in computational and applied mechanics, hardware design and prototyping, and supply chain management.

NOEMI WALZEBUCK | Business Development and Customer Support

Noemi leads Business Development at Summer Technologies and works to develop new partnerships for Summer and keep our customers happy! She previously worked at The William and Flora Hewlett Foundation analyzing U.S. energy policy, the World Resources Institute researching subsistence agroforestry, and the Environmental Defense Fund aiding ecosystem service policy creation. She received a B.S. in Environmental Science and an M.S. in Agriculture and Natural Resources Management from Stanford.

GAURAV GAMPA | Android Developer

Gaurav Gampa is a Mobile Application Developer (Intern) at Summer Technologies. He is currently pursuing his graduate studies at San Jose State University in the field of Software Engineering. His prime interests rest around developing web and mobile applications to make user's lives easy. He is a table tennis player and also likes to play racquetball, cricket.

DAPHENY LEE | UI/UX Designer and Web Developer

Dapheny is a UI/UX designer and web developer at Summer Technologies, and works in creating the user interface (UI) for Summer's products. She ensures that the UI visually communicates the flow from one step to the next, and create the best user experience for our customers.
